ABOUT ME

My name is Mian Wang. I was born in a beautiful small town near the ocean in China. I got my Bachelor in Environmental Sciences from Ocean Univeristy of China. Then, I went to Cornell University and graduated with a Master of Science in Environmental Engineering. Upon graduation, I received an internship offer from Santa Clara Valley Water District. So I moved to California. California is my lucky place. I met my husband and we quickly got married. I consider marrying my husband as my greatest achievement, and my publication in Water Research as the second.
